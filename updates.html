Labor Law Poster Updates

2023 holds a host of labor compliance updates, in fact there were over 30 at the state and federal level and another 30 updates at the city/county level. Most of the updates affect minimum wage rates due to annual CPI (consumer price index) increases. In addition to those state specific updates, all 2023 posters will include the recent October EEOC update. The new Know Your Rights: Workplace Discrimination is Illegal poster updates and replaces the Equal Employment Opportunity is the Law poster.

Businesses operating in regions affected by new labor laws are required to update the latest labor law posters. We've compiled updates across every state and city below. Navigate to your business location below and simply order updated posters to be in compliance.

At the LaborLawCenter™, we monitor every single labor law change for you, so it's easy for your business to stay in compliance.

FEDERAL UPDATES

EEOC Know Your Rights: Workplace Discrimination is Illegal (Updates and Replaces EEO is the Law Poster) - Updated 10/2022 Effective 10/2022

Revised to use plain language and bullet points to summarize the laws for employees. Added a QR code that links to instructions on how to file a charge of workplace discrimination. This new version replaces the old Equal Employment Opportunity is the law poster.

Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) Effective 01/2015

Amendment expands the military family leave provisions and incorporates a special eligibility provision for airline flight crew employees, as well as making some additional regulatory changes..
